Pratigyanam The Healing Source.

The Healing Source is a holistic wellness company that provides music therapy with powerful affirmations to help people balance their chakras and heal themselves. Our music therapy sessions are designed to help you relax and de-stress, open and balance your chakras, and heal your body and mind. We use music therapy with the correct sound frequencies to create powerful affirmations that will resonate with your chakras and help you to heal.

We also have a 21-day course that teaches you how to balance your chakras and heal your body and mind.

We are committed to providing our clients with the highest quality holistic wellness services. We use only the most effective methods and techniques, and we are always learning and growing to ensure that we are providing the best possible care for our clients.

If you are ready to start your journey towards holistic well-being, we invite you to join our community at Pratigyanam: The Healing Source. We would be honored to be a part of your journey towards holistic wellness.
